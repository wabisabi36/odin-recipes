Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are soft, buttery cookies loaded with sweet chocolate chips. They’re slightly crisp on the edges, chewy in the center, and a classic homemade treat enjoyed warm from the oven.


Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)

    2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    3. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.

  3. Cream butter and sugars

    4. In a large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add eggs and vanilla

    5. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

  5. Combine

    6. Gradually mix in dry ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips.

  6. Bake

    7. Drop tablespoon-sized dough balls onto baking sheet, spacing 2 inches apart.

    Bake 8–10 minutes, until edges are golden and centers look slightly soft.

  7. Cool

    8. Let sit on the baking sheet 5 minutes before transferring to a rack.
