Baked Potato Casserole

Description

A creamy, cheesy comfort-food casserole that combines tender potatoes with a golden breadcrumb topping—perfect for weeknight dinners or holiday sides.


Ingredients

  • 6 large russet potatoes (about 3 lbs), peeled and diced
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (divided)
  • 6 slices cooked bacon, crumbled (optional)
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)

    2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  2. Cook the potatoes

    3. Place diced potatoes in a large pot, cover with salted water, and boil for 15–20 minutes until fork-tender. Drain well.

  3. Mash & mix

    4. In a large bowl, mash potatoes slightly (leave some chunks for texture).

    Stir in sour cream, milk, melted butter, garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, 1½ cups cheddar cheese, bacon (if using), and half the green onions.

  4. Assemble

    5. Spread mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.

    Top with remaining ½ cup cheese.

  5. Bake

    6. Bake uncovered for 20–25 minutes until heated through and cheese is bubbly and lightly golden.

  6. Garnish & serve

    7. Sprinkle remaining green onions on top before serving.
